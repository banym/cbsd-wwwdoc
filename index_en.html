About the project

CBSD is a wrapper from sh-scripts (mainly) round a jail(8) subsystem an operating system FreeBSD OS. Any new functional in OS at this stage it is not brought - everything that can do cbsd scripts, you can do by command (by commands, tens commands, hundreds of commands) in CLI through the corresponding utilities. The project becomes as for relief of own needs on administration of jails and servers, and 'just for fun'.   More...
